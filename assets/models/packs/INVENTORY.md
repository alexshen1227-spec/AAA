# Model Pack Inventory

Curated free packs for The Wilds of Aerwyn (stylized low-poly, toon-shaded).
All files verified: GLB magic + JSON chunk parse OK; all .gltf external URIs
(bin/textures) present. Tri counts are index-buffer estimates.
Licenses: everything CC0 except the two `*_googlepoly.glb` files (CC-BY 3.0) —
see `assets/licenses/MODEL-CREDITS.md`.

Scale note: Kenney kits are authored around 1 unit = 1 tile (small; scale up
~2-4x in world). KayKit dungeon pieces snap to a 4x4 grid at 1u. Quaternius
MegaKit models are roughly real-scale (trees 5-10u tall).
